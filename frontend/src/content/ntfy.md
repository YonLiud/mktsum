# Setting up notifications

We use [ntfy](https://ntfy.sh) to deliver your daily briefings straight to your phone — no accounts, no fuss. Here's how to get it going in a couple of minutes.

## 1. Download the app

Grab ntfy from the App Store or Google Play — it's free and open source.

- [iOS — App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/ntfy/id1625396347)
- [Android — Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=io.heckel.ntfy)

## 2. Pick a topic name

A "topic" is just a unique name for your notification channel — think of it like a private mailbox. It can be anything you like: your username, a random word, whatever feels right. Just make it something others won't accidentally guess, since anyone who knows the topic name can subscribe to it.

Good examples: `alex-mktsum-7x2`, `morning-briefings-jana`, `mktsum-{your name}`

## 3. Subscribe in the app

Open ntfy, tap **+**, and enter your topic name. That's it — you're subscribed and ready to receive.

## 4. Add it to your profile

Head over to your [profile](/profile) in mktsum and paste your topic name into the **ntfy topic** field. Hit save, and you're all set.

From that point on, your daily briefing will land on your phone each morning — full summary, short TL;DR, and all.

---

**That's really all there is to it.** If something isn't working, double-check that the topic in your profile exactly matches what you subscribed to in the app (it's case-sensitive). Feel free to reach out if you get stuck.
